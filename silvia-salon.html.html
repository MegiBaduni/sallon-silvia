Welcome to Silvia Salon & Spa

Where beauty
meets luxury

Nestled in the heart of Sarandë on Albania's stunning Riviera, Silvia Salon & Spa is a sanctuary crafted exclusively for women. Under the expert care of Silvia Goxha, every visit is a curated experience — not just a treatment.

From transformative hair colour and extensions to sculpted nails, lifted lashes, and glowing skin — we bring together the finest techniques and the warmth of true Albanian hospitality.
